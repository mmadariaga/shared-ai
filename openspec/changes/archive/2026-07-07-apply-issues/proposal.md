> **⚠ POST-HOC RECORD** — This proposal was backfilled after implementation. It describes a decision already made, not one being proposed.

## Why

The apply workflow needed to keep running until the whole plan was finished, not stop after a single step, and it needed a stricter completion definition to avoid reporting success before all checkboxes and the final sweep were done. The prompt also needed tighter limits to reduce unnecessary exploration and cost.

## What Changes

- `sai/commands/sai-4-apply.md`: changes the completion message to require all steps, all human verification gates, and all commits before declaring success.
- `sai/instructions/apply.md`: adds a loop that returns to the next unchecked step until the plan is complete.
- `sai/instructions/apply.md`: tightens the subagent prompt so it only contains the step text, the rules, and relevant technical learnings.
- `sai/instructions/apply.md`: adds a no-exploration rule and forbids reading change artifacts or running `openspec` commands from the step dispatch prompt.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

None.

### Modified Capabilities

- `apply-completion-clarity`: completion now requires the final sweep and every step checkbox to be checked.
- `apply-step-delegation`: step dispatch now loops through all unchecked steps instead of stopping after one.
- `apply-final-sweep`: the final sweep is now a completion gate, not just a post-hoc check.
- `apply-coordinator-authority`: the coordinator now keeps subagent prompts self-contained and blocks exploratory context gathering.
